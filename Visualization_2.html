City Latitude vs. Humidity

When observing the scatterplot of city latitude vs humidity, there seems to be a cluster of cities at humidity levels above 60% on this day, but a weak relationship with latitude except for a small chart area around the equator with no cities represented until you rise to around 50% humidity and above. This could mean that areas around the equator inside of the -10 to 10 degrees latitude bounds are generally drier. Again, on this chart the cities are skewed towards the global north.
